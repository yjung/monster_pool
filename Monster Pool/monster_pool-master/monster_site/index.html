Controls

The controls for the game are quite simple:

  • Just press the right click of the mouse or touchpad and move around to see your surroundings.
  • In order to change the point where you hit the ball, just press the right button of your mouse or touchpad and move it to move the queue.
  • X – Press this key to hit the ball

About

Monster Pool is a web based game developed for the lecture of Computer Games in the University of Applied Sciences Fulda. There are beside balls, some monsters and obstacles in order to make the game more interesting. The goal of the game is to put all monsters and balls in the holes. You can get a different quantity of points depending on the hole you aim to. The points are distributed in the following way:

  • You get 30 points, if you aim to the holes behind the slider.
  • 10 points if you aim to the center holes.
  • You get also the great quantity of 30 points if you aim to the holes that are surrounded by bumpes and accessible through the ramp.
